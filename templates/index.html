Adult Height Prediction

Adult height depends on various factors, including genetics, family background, and lifestyle. This project looks beyond simple parental influence to include elements like birth order, daily activities, diet, and living conditions, creating a more detailed and reliable way to predict how tall someone will grow.

By studying a wide range of influences, this project offers a new approach to understanding growth patterns. It emphasizes the importance of clean and accurate data while considering the many factors that shape height. This work aims to provide families and healthcare professionals with better insights for tracking and supporting a child's growth.
